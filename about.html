About IIT Delhi

Established in 1961, the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi (IIT Delhi) is one of the most prestigious and leading technical institutes in India. It stands at the forefront of innovation, education, and research. IIT Delhi is committed to producing highly skilled engineers, scientists, and technologists who contribute to societal development globally.

With state-of-the-art infrastructure, diverse programs, and outstanding faculty, IIT Delhi ensures that every student not only receives academic excellence but also develops holistically to tackle challenges in the rapidly changing technological world.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To be a global leader in technological education and research, nurturing engineers and scientists who will shape the future of innovation, industry, and society.

Mission: To create an inclusive and world-class learning environment that encourages students to think critically, act ethically, and develop skills necessary to succeed in an ever-evolving world.

Our History

IIT Delhi was founded in 1961 with the aim of providing high-quality education and research in the fields of engineering and technology. Over the decades, IIT Delhi has grown into a globally recognized institute with a rich legacy of excellence in education, research, and innovation.

Initially, the institute offered only undergraduate programs. However, with the increasing demand for technical education, IIT Delhi expanded its offerings to include postgraduate programs and research opportunities, now recognized as one of the top institutions for higher learning in the country.

Our Leadership

IIT Delhi is led by a team of visionary leaders, including its Director, Deans, and faculty members, who are all dedicated to maintaining the institute’s global standards of education and research.

The leadership works collaboratively with students, faculty, and industry experts to ensure that IIT Delhi remains at the forefront of technological advancements, providing students with the best possible opportunities for academic and professional success.

Academic Excellence

IIT Delhi offers a range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines of engineering, technology, and management. With a rigorous curriculum, a wide array of specializations, and cutting-edge research, IIT Delhi fosters an environment where students are empowered to innovate, experiment, and solve real-world problems.

Undergraduate Programs

  • Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in various fields like Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Civil Engineering, etc.
  • Bachelor of Design (B.Des) and Bachelor of Management Studies (BMS).

Postgraduate Programs

  • Master of Technology (M.Tech) in specialized fields.
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA).
  • Master of Design (M.Des).
  • Master of Science (M.Sc) in various scientific fields.

Doctoral Programs

PhD programs in multiple disciplines, enabling students to engage in high-impact research with global significance.

Our Students

IIT Delhi is home to a vibrant and diverse student body comprising individuals from all across India and beyond. Students here are not only focused on academics but also participate in a wide range of extracurricular activities, including cultural festivals, technical events, sports, and community outreach programs.

The student community at IIT Delhi is deeply involved in global research, innovation, and leadership initiatives, with numerous students winning national and international awards for their research and contributions.

Campus Life

IIT Delhi boasts a beautiful campus filled with modern infrastructure, laboratories, libraries, sports complexes, hostels, and recreational spaces. The campus is designed to foster both academic and personal growth for students.

The institute offers several student clubs and societies that focus on academics, arts, sports, culture, and social issues. Whether you're into robotics, theater, dance, or social activism, there's always something happening on campus.

Global Impact

IIT Delhi’s research and innovation have global implications in technology, science, and industry. Our faculty and students collaborate with international universities, research labs, and industries to solve global challenges.

Some notable initiatives include clean energy solutions, AI and robotics, and healthcare advancements, all aimed at improving the quality of life worldwide.
