Academic Departments

Computer Science and Engineering

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at IIT Delhi is one of the most renowned in the country, providing cutting-edge research and teaching in various aspects of computer science, including artificial intelligence, machine learning, data science, cybersecurity, and software engineering.

Electrical Engineering

The Department of Electrical Engineering offers programs that cover a wide range of disciplines, including communications, power systems, control systems, and electronics. Research in this department focuses on advancing technologies like renewable energy, smart grids, and telecommunications.

Mechanical Engineering

The Department of Mechanical Engineering provides students with a deep understanding of mechanics, dynamics, thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and manufacturing. It is also at the forefront of research in robotics, automation, and sustainable energy technologies.

Civil Engineering

The Department of Civil Engineering at IIT Delhi focuses on infrastructure development, sustainable construction, and environmental engineering. Research includes topics such as smart cities, structural engineering, and transportation systems.

Chemical Engineering

The Department of Chemical Engineering specializes in chemical process engineering, material science, and biochemical engineering. Research in this field aims to solve global challenges such as clean energy production, pollution control, and sustainable chemical processes.

Department of Design

The Department of Design focuses on interdisciplinary design education with an emphasis on product design, industrial design, and interaction design. It integrates design thinking with technology to create innovative solutions for society.
